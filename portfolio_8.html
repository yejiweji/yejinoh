Last Train Home
2021
Medium: Procreate
Process and intention: I have been interested in video games for as long as I can remember. One of my earliest memories is being absolutely mesmerised playing Tak 2: The Staff of Dreams on the GameCube with my siblings. I have a dream of one day being able to share this joy and escape with other children by creating games of my own. This piece is concept art for my in-progress puzzle-adventure game about a lost cat finding their way back to their owner by navigating a city’s subway system. Challenges arise as the player who plays in the perspective of the cat must find food, evade road hazards, be mindful of the weather and most importantly, avoid getting trapped by the city’s animal patrol.
