The Exhibition
2022
Medium: Procreate, Github, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery
Process and intention: You can find the code here. I welcome you to The Yejin Oh Exhibition, now open for VIPs, free of charge! As portfolios may feel redundant, I wanted to create my own website to display my work using Github Pages. I decided to include this as one of my 10 pieces as I am able to showcase a combination of my digital art skills, and my knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. All visual graphics including the background, podium, poles, light fixtures and the front desk, were designed and drawn through Procreate. I intend for the experience to be similar to as if you were “walking” and viewing artworks in an actual art gallery, hence why the website is a continuous horizontal scroll. I included UX elements that may make your experience more convenient such as, linking each artwork in the table of contents(Current Exhibition) on the front page, with their corresponding location in the gallery, and the “EXIT” sign above the door on the last page will bring you back to the front page once it is clicked.
