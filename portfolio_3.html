Not Myself
2022
Medium: Acrylic paint on canvas
Process and intention: Not Myself is an impactful and thought-provoking experience. Revolving around the subject of identity and the struggle to feel comfortable as our most authentic self, forcing us to conjure up multiple different personas. Inspired by artist Basquiat, I included two images of skulls to allude to the “fake” personalities we may put up. It’s to say that those personas are parts of you that could “die” yet would still not take away from your “true self”. I intend for the frustration, the anger, and the defeat I felt in regards to figuring out who I am and what my purpose is, to be felt by the viewer. I effectively achieved this through the use of splatters, rough paint strokes, use of text, and the expression of my main subject. Overall, I was able to achieve a very passionate piece that embodies Neo Expressionism by combining elements of realism and expressing them in a rough way, using vivid colours and bold lines
