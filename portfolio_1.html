Swimmer
Medium: Procreate
Process and intention: This piece was drawn in the thick of winter when I was heavily missing the summer warmth and the fun outdoor activities that come along with the heat. What reminds one more of summer than a day at the pool, so I decided to draw what I missed most as I explored an illustrative style. Through the use of patterns, and simple yet contrasting colours; red and blue, I was able to achieve a minimalist aesthetic whilst maintaining variety and interest in my piece. Intentionally, I incorporated black and white to draw attention to the main subject of my piece and to ensure that they are not overpowered by the background. Overall, I intend for my piece to communicate the refreshing coolness we feel after spending time in a pool.
