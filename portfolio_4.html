Sick Of Fruit
2020
Medium: Terracotta clay, Acrylic paint
Process and intention: Mr . W. Melon is a pottery project I created back when I first learnt to work with ceramics in 2018. Created with terracotta clay, baked in a kiln, and then painted, it was entirely a new experience. Since then, my love for pottery continues and I’ve created a plethora of other small plates and trinkets using air-dry clay as this is more accessible as a casual hobby. Mr . W. Melon’s design is inspired by a watermelon as it’s vibrant and contrasting colours seemed like an ideal choice for this contemporary piece. Additionally, I hoped to create a mug that was out of the box and not often seen, so I decided to personify a fruit. I chose to give my mug a goofy and relaxed expression and a reddened nose to communicate the drowsy feeling after taking medicine when you’re sick to match how I was feeling at the time, as I was ill with the flu.
