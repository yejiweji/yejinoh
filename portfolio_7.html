Miraculous
Medium: Blender
Process and intention: As I progressed in my artistic and creative journey, I felt that it was most appropriate to dabble in 3D graphics next. I created a bust of a male character that could possibly be featured in children's 3D animated television programs such as Miraculous: Tales of Ladybug & Cat Noir. This is a show I greatly enjoyed when I was younger and currently my little cousin’s favourite. Prince Eric from Disney’s animated film, The Little Mermaid, served as my main inspiration in designing this new character. I chose Prince Eric as he resembles my cousin and there is a lack of dark haired male characters in Miraculous: Tales of Ladybug & Cat Noir. I wanted to create a character children, like my cousin, could heavily associate themselves with and it was overall easily done with minimal challenges.
